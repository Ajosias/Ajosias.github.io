Guide to the Slack Side Bar

1. Workspace - Click to reveal section for selecting workspace.
2. New Message Button - Click to bring up message comosition section.
3. Threads - Click to reveal all your current communications.
4. All DMs - Click to reveal all your direct messages.
5. Mentions & reactions - Click to reveal all communications that tag you.
6. Saved items - Click to reveal all items you've saved.
7. Slack Connect - Not used by WTC.
8. More - Click to reveal list of lesser used functions.
9. Channels - Click to reveal list of all channels you've joined.
10. Priority - Click to reveal list of important people you've added for quick access.
11. Direct messages - Click to reveal list of people you have exchanged messages with.
12. Apps - Click to reveal list of the additional apps you've added to Slack.
