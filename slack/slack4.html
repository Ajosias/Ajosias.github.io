When do I use Slack?
You must check Slack multiple times per day. It should be one of the first things you do everyday, and also the last. This means that the latest you should reply to any communication is the next morning. Keeping people waiting for a response to work related matters is not acceptable and will not be toleratred. Claiming not to have seen a communication is not a valid excuse. Remember that Slack is not free - WTC pays a considerable amount to make this application available for your avantage, so you need to make full use of it. Make sure that you join all relevant channels, stay up to date with communications, and be diligent in checking for direct messages from your peers, and especially from staff members.
