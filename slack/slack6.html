Slack etiquette
Although you may use slack for private communications, and occasionally communications that are not strictly work related, it is important to remember that professional standards ALWAYS apply. This means that your language, tone, writing, and subject matter MUST be of a standard to suit a professional workplace. No swearing, rudeness, lack of respect or tollerance, non-PC or controversial subjects, or any bad behaviour is EVER acceptable. WTC is here to prepare you for the workplace, thus anything that could get you sent to the Human Resources department in a workplace is liable to have you rempoved from WTC without warning. As a rule of thumb - if you wouldn't say it to your grandmother, don't type it into Slack.
